A Few Words About Our Sahasra College

Manepalli Venkateswara Rao, our esteemed founder, brings 33+ years of teaching experience from both corporate and Government colleges.

His vision for Sahasra is to provide a nurturing environment for students to pursue their academic goals and contribute meaningfully to society.

Venkatesawara Rao Manepalli's dedication to education led to the establishment of Sahasra in 2019 with a modest beginning of 49 students.

As a visionary leader and educator, he believes in the transformative power of education to shape the future generations.

Sahasra stands as a beacon of knowledge and innovation, inspired by the founder's commitment to excellence and lifelong learning.


Sahasra AIM

Sahasra College aims to provide students with a holistic education that goes beyond academics, focusing on their overall development, including intellectual, emotional, and social growth.

Our aim is to prepare students for successful careers by equipping them with the necessary skills, knowledge, and experiences needed to excel in their chosen fields.

Sahasra College strives to foster a sense of community among its students, encouraging them to actively engage in societal issues and contribute positively to their communities.
